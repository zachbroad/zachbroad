### First impressions matter.

Our highly skilled team of designers can help you prepare your brand for its digital presence with a beautiful, eye-catching logo.


## The Process
Having a meaningful logo for your business is important.
### Consultation
I will meet with you to discuss ideas for your logo. 
We will discuss the following:
- Examples of logos you like
- Color scheme 
- What you want your logo to "stand for"
### Mock-up
Once we find a rough idea of what you are looking for in a logo, we will make a few iterations on the idea.
### Revisions
Here is where you can make changes to your logo before the final product.
### Finalizing
Your new logo is ready to be used all over the web!
