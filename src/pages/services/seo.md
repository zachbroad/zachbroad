## Stand out among your competitors on Google

### What is SEO?
Search engine optimization, commonly abbreviated as _SEO_, is the process of ranking your website for specific, targeted keywords. 
SEO is the reason why you're able to find what you're looking for on Google.

Without SEO, your website will struggle to be discovered. It's important that a website is optimized in a way that search engines can easily "crawl" the pages to find what people are looking for. 


### Basic SEO Package
Standard with all of my websites is a basic SEO package. This includes...

- On-page SEO
    - This includes things such as the page titles, meta descriptions, etc.
- Technical SEO
    - Includes proper use of HTML5 tags, mobile friendliness, HTTPS, and ensuring there are no errors such as broken links on your site. 
- Google Analytics 
    - Google Analytics is a great tool to learn more about your website's audience. 
- Google Search Console
    - Google Search Console is another tool that you can use to monitor your website's performance on Google.
