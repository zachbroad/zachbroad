### What is SEO?

Search engine optimization — SEO — is a marketing discipline focused on **growing visibility in organic (non-paid) search engine results**.

SEO encompasses both the technical and creative elements required to improve rankings, drive traffic, and increase awareness in search engines.

###PRPL Pattern

PRPL is a web site architecture developed by Google for building websites and apps. All of my websites are designed with this in mind. 

Google was developed PRPL in response to the mobile web being too slow — it works exceptionally well on smartphones and other **devices with unreliable network connections**.


PRPL stands for:

* **Push critical resources** for the initial URL route using <link preload> and http/2.
* **Render** initial route.
* **Pre-cache** remaining routes.
* **Lazy-load** and create remaining routes on demand.

### What does it do?

Basically, your website will load **much faster**, especially when your internet connection is slow.

With [most web traffic nowadays coming from mobile devices](https://www.statista.com/statistics/241462/global-mobile-phone-website-traffic-share/), it's important for websites to be designed in a way that allows users with sub-par service to still access your page.
